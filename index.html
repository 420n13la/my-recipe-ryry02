licorne soup recipe

Introduction

This delicious soup can be served with juice or water. This recipe produces 25 soup.

Ingredients

  • 1 licorne
  • 100ml water
  • 250g vegetable and licorne's meet

Steps

  1. Reheat the oven between 250 and 350℃
  2. Reduce licorne's meet to cube
  3. Add vegetables, water and licorne's meet white
  4. form small cube of 4 to 5cm
  5. Back for 45min
